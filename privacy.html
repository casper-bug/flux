PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: April 2026

1. DATA STORAGE

Flux is a client-side application. We do not own or operate any servers to store your files or links. All data is stored directly in your own Google Drive account within a dedicated folder called "FluxSpace".

2. GOOGLE DRIVE ACCESS

Flux uses the drive.file scope. This means the application can only see and access files that it specifically created or that you have explicitly opened with it. It cannot read your other personal files in Google Drive.

3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information. Your Google account profile information (name, picture) is fetched via the Google Identity Services solely to display it in the app's user interface and is stored only on your local device (localStorage).

4. ANALYTICS & TRACKING

There are no third-party analytics, trackers, or cookies used in this application.

5. CONTACT

Since Flux is an open-source tool, for any concerns regarding the privacy logic, you can inspect the source code on GitHub.
